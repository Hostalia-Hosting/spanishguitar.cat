Costa Brava & Empordà Series

Wild Beauty & Authentic Heritage

The Costa Brava is synonymous with rugged cliffs, turquoise waters, and ancient stone "Masias." These sessions are recorded in the heart of the Empordà, where the music resonates with the natural beauty of the landscape. These videos highlight the versatility of the Spanish guitar in open-air environments and rustic-chic celebrations.

  • Atmosphere: Authentic, wild, and prestigious.
  • Ideal for: Cliffside "I do" moments, traditional Catalan Masias, and garden appetizers.
  • Musical Style: Pure Spanish guitar essence mixed with acoustic renditions of iconic love songs.
