Barcelona Sessions

Urban Luxury & Historic Elegance

Barcelona is a city where Gothic history meets contemporary luxury. My music in the city is designed to complement its unique architectural soul. Whether it is a rooftop reception overlooking the skyline at the Hotel W or an intimate ceremony in a hidden Gothic Quarter courtyard, these videos showcase a sophisticated repertoire.

  • Atmosphere: Chic, cosmopolitan, and timeless.
  • Ideal for: Luxury hotel weddings, historic palaces, and urban terraces.
  • Musical Style: A blend of modern pop instrumental covers and classical Spanish masterpieces.
