Sitges & Penedès Collections

Sunset Vibes & Vineyard Romance

Sitges and the Penedès region offer a unique blend of coastal charm and vineyard elegance. My music in these locations captures the essence of their distinctive landscapes and cultural heritage. Whether it is a romantic evening by the sea or a sophisticated celebration in a historic vineyard, these videos showcase a refined repertoire.

  • Atmosphere: Charming, relaxed, and timeless.
  • Ideal for: Private luxury villas (like Almiral de la Font or Masia Cabellut), beachfront ceremonies, and winery receptions.
  • Musical Style: A blend of modern pop instrumental covers and classical Spanish masterpieces.
